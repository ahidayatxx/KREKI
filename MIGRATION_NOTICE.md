## 🔄 What Changed?

### New Multi-Repository Structure

The previous single-repository approach has been replaced with a **multi-repository ecosystem** that better separates concerns:

1. **Standards & Reference** (Public, Open Source)
   - National standards that other organizations can freely adopt
   - Licensed under CC BY 4.0, MIT, or Apache 2.0

2. **Platform Services** (Source Available, Licensed)
   - KREKI-branded platform implementations
   - Licensed under KREKI Source Available License (KSAL)

3. **Organization-Specific** (Private)
   - KREKI-specific implementations and assets
   - Proprietary, not publicly accessible

### Enhanced Governance

The new organization structure includes:
- **Three-tier licensing model** for appropriate IP protection
- **Formal governance framework** with Steering Committee
- **RFC process** for standards development
- **Clear contribution guidelines** and code review standards
