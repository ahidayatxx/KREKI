**ID:** INACRC-SOP-302
**Versi:** 1.0
**Tanggal Berlaku:** 1 Januari 2026
**Tanggal Tinjau:** 31 Desember 2026

## 1. TUJUAN
Menetapkan alur untuk merencanakan, melaksanakan, dan mengevaluasi program pelatihan Good Clinical Practice (GCP) bagi peneliti, staf penelitian, dan regulator untuk memastikan pemahaman dan penerapan standar GCP yang konsisten dalam pelaksanaan uji klinis di Indonesia.

## 2. RUANG LINGKUP
SOP ini berlaku untuk:
- Tim Pelatihan GCP INA-CRC
- Instruktur dan fasilitator pelatihan
- Principal Investigator, Study Coordinator, dan Research Staff
- Regulator dari Badan POM dan Ditjen Farmalkes
- Komite Etik Penelitian
- Clinical Research Unit (CRU) dan rumah sakit jejaring
- Sponsor dan industry representatives

## 3. DEFINISI
- **Good Clinical Practice (GCP)**: Standar etika dan kualitas internasional untuk uji klinis
- **GCP Training**: Program pelatihan formal untuk pemahaman dan penerapan GCP
- **Certification**: Proses sertifikasi kompetensi GCP yang diakui
- **Training Needs Assessment**: Identifikasi kebutuhan pelatihan spesifik
- **Competency Assessment**: Evaluasi pemahaman dan kemampuan aplikasi GCP
- **Continuous Professional Development**: Pembelajaran berkelanjutan untuk mempertahankan kompetensi

## 4. REFERENSI
- ICH-GCP E6(R2) Guidelines
- KMK Nomor 1458 Tahun 2023 tentang Tata Kelola Uji Klinis
- WHO Guidelines for Good Clinical Practice
- International Conference on Harmonisation (ICH) Guidelines
- Local Regulatory Requirements dan Guidelines

## 6. JENIS PELATIHAN GCP

### 6.1 Basic GCP Training
**Target Audience:** Peneliti pemula, staf penelitian baru, research coordinators
**Duration:** 2 hari (16 jam)
**Coverage:**
- Introduction to clinical research dan GCP principles
- Ethical considerations dan patient protection
- Informed consent process
- Protocol compliance dan source documentation
- Adverse event reporting
- Data integrity dan confidentiality
- Regulatory requirements
- Practical implementation tips

### 6.2 Advanced GCP Training
**Target Audience:** Experienced investigators, study managers, CRU leadership
**Duration:** 3 hari (24 jam)
**Coverage:**
- Complex study design dan implementation
- Advanced safety monitoring dan pharmacovigilance
- Biologics dan advanced therapy products
- Device trials specific requirements
- Regulatory strategies dan compliance
- Risk-based approach dalam GCP
- Quality management systems
- Leadership dalam clinical research

### 6.3 Specialized GCP Training
**Target Audience:** Specific role-based training
**Duration:** 1-2 hari (8-16 jam)
**Coverage:**
- **Data Management**: Electronic data capture, database management
- **Site Management**: Site operations, recruitment strategies
- **Safety Monitoring**: Pharmacovigilance, safety reporting
- **Regulatory Affairs**: Regulatory submissions, compliance
- **Quality Assurance**: Auditing, monitoring, quality systems
- **Device Trials**: Medical device specific GCP requirements

### 6.4 Refresher Training
**Target Audience:** Certified professionals needing update
**Duration:** 1 hari (8 jam)
**Coverage:**
- Updates pada GCP guidelines dan regulations
- Recent regulatory changes dan implications
- Lessons learned dari recent audits/inspections
- Emerging issues dalam clinical research
- Best practices sharing
- Case studies dari recent experiences

## 7. PROSEDUR PENYELENGGARAAN PELATIHAN

### 7.1 Training Needs Assessment
**Waktu:** Triwulan sebelum periode pelatihan
1. **Koordinator Pelatihan GCP**:
   - Melakukan Training Needs Assessment:
     * Survey kompetensi GCP saat ini
     * Identifikasi gap antara current dan required competencies
     * Analisis regulatory requirements changes
     * Review audit findings dan compliance issues
     * Assess stakeholder requirements dan expectations

   - Mengembangkan Training Plan:
     * Prioritize training needs berdasarkan impact dan urgency
     * Tentukan target audience untuk setiap program
     * Estimate resource requirements (instruktur, venue, materials)
     * Develop training calendar dengan specific dates
     * Identify budget requirements dan funding sources

### 7.2 Kurikulum Development
**Waktu:** 1-2 bulan sebelum pelatihan
1. **Lead Instructor**:
   - Mengembangkan comprehensive curriculum:
     * Learning objectives untuk setiap module
     * Detailed course outline dengan time allocation
     * Teaching methods (lectures, workshops, case studies, role-plays)
     * Assessment methods dan evaluation criteria
     * Reference materials dan reading lists
     * Practical exercises dan assignments

   - Quality assurance review:
     * Internal review oleh senior GCP experts
     * Alignment dengan international GCP standards
     * Relevance dengan Indonesian context dan regulations
     * Practical applicability untuk participants
     * Update dengan recent developments dalam field

### 7.3 Instruktur Selection dan Preparation
**Waktu:** 1 bulan sebelum pelatihan
1. **Koordinator Pelatihan GCP**:
   - Selection criteria untuk instructors:
     * Extensive experience dalam clinical research
     * GCP certification atau equivalent expertise
     * Teaching dan presentation skills
     * Up-to-date knowledge dengan current regulations
     * Ability to engage participants effectively

   - Instructor preparation:
     * Briefing session tentang training objectives
     * Review curriculum dan teaching materials
     * Coordination session untuk multi-instructor programs
     * Training delivery techniques dan best practices
     * Contingency planning untuk technical issues

### 7.4 Registrasi dan Logistik
**Waktu:** 2-4 minggu sebelum pelatihan
1. **Training Administrator**:
   - Participant registration:
     * Open registration dengan clear requirements
     * Screening participants untuk appropriate background
     * Confirmation emails dengan detailed information
     * Waiting list management untuk over-subscribed programs
     * Special accommodation requests handling

   - Logistics preparation:
     * Venue booking dengan appropriate facilities
     * Audio-visual equipment setup dan testing
     * Training materials preparation (handouts, certificates)
     * Catering arrangements (coffee breaks, lunch)
     * Name tags, attendance sheets, evaluation forms

### 7.5 Pelaksanaan Pelatihan
**Waktu:** Sesuai durasi pelatihan
1. **Lead Instructor**:
   - Opening session (Day 1 - 1 hour):
     * Welcome dan introductions
     * Training objectives dan agenda review
     * Participant expectations dan ground rules
     * Overview dari assessment methods
     * Administrative information

   - Training delivery (sesuai curriculum):
     * Interactive lectures dengan visual aids
     * Case studies dari real clinical research scenarios
     * Group discussions dan brainstorming sessions
     * Role-play exercises untuk practical application
     * Q&A sessions untuk clarification
     * Knowledge checks dan mini-quizzes

   - Closing session (Final day - 1 hour):
     * Key learning points summary
     * Next steps dan continuous learning
     * Certificate distribution
     * Feedback collection dan evaluation
     * Networking opportunities

2. **Training Administrator**:
   - On-site coordination:
     * Registration desk setup dan participant check-in
     * Venue setup dan equipment management
     * Time management dan schedule adherence
     * Support untuk technical issues
     * Documentation dari attendance dan participation

### 7.6 Assessment dan Sertifikasi
**Waktu:** Selama dan setelah pelatihan
1. **Lead Instructor**:
   - Assessment methods:
     * Written examination (multiple choice, short answer, case study)
     * Practical assessment (role-play, scenario analysis)
     * Group project evaluation
     * Class participation dan engagement
     * Post-training assignment (untuk advanced courses)

   - Scoring dan certification:
     * Minimum passing score: 80% untuk basic, 75% untuk advanced
     * Certificate categories:
       * **GCP Basic Certificate**: Successful completion dari basic course
       * **GCP Advanced Certificate**: Successful completion dari advanced course
       * **GCP Specialist Certificate**: Successful completion dari specialized course
       * **GCP Refresher Certificate**: Completion dari refresher course

     * Certificate validity: 2 years dengan renewal requirements

### 7.7 Evaluasi Pelatihan
**Waktu:** 1 minggu setelah pelatihan
1. **Training Administrator**:
   - Collect evaluation data:
     * Participant feedback forms (Lampiran A)
     * Instructor self-evaluation
     * Observer assessment (jika applicable)
     * Post-training assessment results
     * Follow-up survey setelah 3 bulan

   - Analyze evaluation results:
     * Identify strengths dari training program
     * Pinpoint areas untuk improvement
     * Assess achievement dari learning objectives
     * Measure participant satisfaction
     * Evaluate instructor effectiveness

### 7.8 Pelaporan dan Follow-Up
**Waktu:** 2-4 minggu setelah pelatihan
1. **Koordinator Pelatihan GCP**:
   - Generate Training Report (Lampiran B):
     * Executive summary dengan key findings
     * Participant demographics dan background
     * Training outcomes dan assessment results
     * Evaluation summary dan recommendations
     * Resource utilization dan cost analysis
     * Future training needs identification

   - Follow-up activities:
     * Distribute certificates dan contact information
     * Share training materials dan resources
     * Schedule refresher training announcements
     * Monitor participant application dalam workplace
     * Collect feedback dari supervisors/managers

## 8. KOMPETENSI GCP YANG DITRAGETKAN

### 8.1 Core GCP Competencies
- **Ethical Principles**: Understanding dari ethical principles dalam clinical research
- **Patient Protection**: Knowledge tentang patient rights dan welfare
- **Informed Consent**: Ability untuk conduct proper informed consent process
- **Protocol Compliance**: Understanding protocol adherence的重要性
- **Documentation**: Proper source documentation dan record keeping
- **Safety Reporting**: Knowledge tentang adverse event reporting requirements
- **Data Integrity**: Understanding data accuracy dan confidentiality requirements
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Knowledge tentang relevant regulations dan guidelines

### 8.2 Advanced GCP Competencies
- **Study Design**: Complex study design dan implementation
- **Risk Management**: Risk-based approach dalam clinical research
- **Quality Systems**: Quality management dalam clinical research
- **Regulatory Strategy**: Advanced regulatory compliance strategies
- **Leadership**: Leadership dalam clinical research teams
- **Troubleshooting**: Problem-solving dalam clinical research challenges

## 9. SERTIFIKASI DAN RENEWAL

### 9.1 Certification Process
1. **Eligibility Requirements**:
   - Minimum educational qualification (health-related degree)
   - Experience dalam clinical research atau related field
   - Completion dari accredited GCP training program
   - Successful completion dari assessment process

2. **Certificate Issuance**:
   - Unique certificate number
   * Participant name dan identification
   * Training level dan completion date
   * Validity period (2 years)
   * Issuing authority (INA-CRC)
   * Verification mechanism

3. **Certificate Renewal**:
   - Completion dari refresher training before expiry
   - Evidence dari continued GCP application
   * No significant compliance issues dalam past 2 years
   * Continuing education credits (minimum 10 hours/year)

### 9.2 Recertification Process
1. **Application Requirements**:
   - Current GCP certificate (belum expired)
   - Evidence dari ongoing clinical research activity
   * Documentation dari continued education
   * No major compliance violations dalam certification period

2. **Renewal Assessment**:
   - Online refresher module completion
   * Updated knowledge assessment
   * Review dari practical application experience
   * Verification dari continuing professional development

## 11. PEMANTAUAN DAN EVALUASI

### 11.1 Key Performance Indicators
- Training completion rate: ≥ 95%
- Assessment passing rate: ≥ 85%
- Participant satisfaction: ≥ 4.2 dari 5.0
- Instructor effectiveness: ≥ 4.3 dari 5.0
- Knowledge retention: ≥ 80% setelah 3 bulan

### 11.2 Monitoring Schedule
- **Per Training**: Real-time monitoring, participant feedback collection
- **Monthly**: Training logistics review, instructor performance evaluation
- **Quarterly**: Training program effectiveness review, curriculum updates
- **Semi-annually**: Instructor competency assessment, program strategy review
- **Annually**: Comprehensive training program evaluation

### 11.3 Evaluation Criteria
- Achievement dari learning objectives
- Participant engagement dan participation
- Quality dari instructional materials
- Effectiveness dari teaching methods
- Practical application dari learned skills

## 13. RISK MANAGEMENT

### 13.1 Training Quality Risks
- **Outdated Content**: Material yang tidak sesuai dengan current regulations
- **Instructor Quality**: Instruktur yang tidak kompeten atau tidak engaging
- **Poor Logistics**: Venue, equipment, atau scheduling issues
- **Assessment Validity**: Assessment yang tidak akurat mengukur kompetensi
- **Participant Engagement**: Low participation atau engagement

### 13.2 Mitigation Strategies
- **Content Review**: Regular review dengan subject matter experts
- **Instructor Selection**: Rigorous selection dan regular evaluation
- **Contingency Planning**: Backup plans untuk technical atau logistical issues
- **Assessment Validation**: Psychometric validation dari assessment tools
- **Interactive Methods**: Use dari engaging teaching techniques
