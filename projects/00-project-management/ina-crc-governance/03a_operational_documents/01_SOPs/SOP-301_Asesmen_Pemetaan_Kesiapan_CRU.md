## 1. TUJUAN
Menetapkan kriteria dan metodologi untuk menilai tingkat kematangan dan kesiapan Clinical Research Unit (CRU) dalam jejaring rumah sakit untuk melaksanakan uji klinis sesuai standar internasional dan regulasi nasional.

## 2. RUANG LINGKUP
SOP ini berlaku untuk:
- Semua rumah sakit yang berminat menjadi atau sudah menjadi CRU
- Tim Asesmen INA-CRC dan Ditjen Kesmas/Keslan
- External assessors dari akademisi atau institusi terkait
- Management rumah sakit yang diassesmen
- Principal Investigator dan tim penelitian di CRU
- Stakeholder terkait dalam pengembangan CRU

## 3. DEFINISI
- **Clinical Research Unit (CRU)**: Unit penelitian klinis di rumah sakit dengan fasilitas dan SDM yang memadai
- **Maturity Level**: Tingkat kematangan CRU berdasarkan kemampuan dan kapasitasnya
- **Capability Assessment**: Penilaian komprehensif terhadap kemampuan CRU
- **Readiness Score**: Skor kesiapan CRU untuk melaksanakan jenis uji klinis tertentu
- **Gap Analysis**: Analisis selisih antara kondisi saat ini dengan standar yang dibutuhkan
- **Development Roadmap**: Rencana pengembangan CRU untuk mencapai target kematangan

## 4. REFERENSI
- KMK Nomor 1458 Tahun 2023 tentang Tata Kelola Uji Klinis
- ICH-GCP E6(R2) Guidelines Section 5
- WHO Guidelines for Good Clinical Practice (GCP)
- ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System
- Hospital Accreditation Standards KARS
- Best Practice Clinical Site Management

## 6. MATURITY LEVEL FRAMEWORK

### 6.1 Level Definitions
| Level | Status | Description | Capability Range |
|-------|--------|-------------|------------------|
| **Level 0** | Not Ready | Tidak memenuhi kriteria minimal uji klinis | Tidak dapat melaksanakan uji klinis |
| **Level 1** | Initial | Kapasitas dasar untuk uji klinis sederhana | Observational, Bioequivalence sederhana |
| **Level 2** | Developing | Kapasitas menengah untuk uji klinis kompleks | Phase II-III, Bioequivalence kompleks |
| **Level 3** | Advanced | Kapasitas lanjut untuk uji klinis kompleks | Multi-center, Device trials |
| **Level 4** | Leading | Kapasitas unggul untuk semua jenis uji klinis | First-in-human, Novel therapeutics |

### 6.2 Assessment Domains
1. **Infrastructure dan Facilities**: Fisik fasilitas dan peralatan
2. **Human Resources**: Kompetensi dan jumlah SDM
3. **Quality Systems**: Prosedur dan sistem manajemen kualitas
4. **Operational Capability**: Kapasitas operasional dan administrasi
5. **Regulatory Compliance**: Kepatuhan terhadap regulasi
6. **Financial Sustainability**: Keberlanjutan finansial

## 7. PROSEDUR ASESMEN

### 7.1 Persiapan Asesmen
**Waktu:** 2-4 minggu sebelum visit asesmen
1. **Koordinator Asesmen**:
   - Mengidentifikasi CRU kandidat untuk asesmen
   - Mengirimkan Assessment Notification Letter (Lampiran A)
   - Menyusun Assessment Schedule dan agenda
   - Menunjuk assessment team (3-5 orang)
   - Menyiapkan Assessment Tools (Lampiran B)

2. **CRU Representative**:
   - Melakukan self-assessment menggunakan Self-Assessment Form (Lampiran C)
   - Menyiapkan dokumen pendukung:
     * Legal documents (akreditasi, perijinan)
     * Organizational structure dan job descriptions
     * CV staff dan sertifikat kompetensi
     * SOPs dan working procedures
     * Quality management system documentation
     * Facilities dan equipment inventory
     * Financial statements dan budget plans
     * Previous clinical trial experiences

### 7.2 Desk Assessment
**Waktu:** 5-7 hari kerja
1. **Assessment Team**:
   - Review submitted documents:
     * Verifikasi kelengkapan dokumen
     * Analisis kualitas dan kedalaman dokumentasi
     * Identifikasi potensi gaps atau issues
     * Penilaian awal terhadap maturity level
     * Persiapan questions untuk on-site visit

   - Melakukan preliminary scoring:
     * Menggunakan Assessment Scoring Matrix (Lampiran D)
     * Memberikan score untuk setiap domain
     * Identifikasi strength dan improvement areas
     * Menyusun preliminary assessment report

### 7.3 On-Site Assessment
**Waktu:** 2-3 hari per CRU
1. **Opening Meeting (Day 1 - 2 hours)**:
   - Introduksi assessment team dan objectives
   - Review agenda dan expected outcomes
   - Confirmation dari schedule dan logistics
   - Overview dari assessment methodology

2. **Facility Tour dan Infrastructure Assessment (Day 1 - 4 hours)**:
   - Clinical facilities (wards, clinics, examination rooms)
   - Laboratory facilities dan equipment
   - Pharmacy dan investigational product storage
   - Data management system dan IT infrastructure
   - Patient facilities dan comfort areas
   - Emergency facilities dan equipment

3. **Document Review (Day 1-2 - 8 hours)**:
   - SOPs dan working procedures
   * Quality management system documentation
   * Training records dan competency assessments
   * Previous audit findings dan corrective actions
   * Financial records dan budget planning
   * Regulatory compliance documentation

4. **Staff Interviews (Day 1-2 - 8 hours)**:
   - Hospital management dan leadership
   * Principal investigators dan researchers
   * Study coordinators dan research staff
   * Laboratory personnel dan pharmacy staff
   * Quality assurance dan administrative staff
   * Data management dan IT staff

5. **System Observation (Day 2 - 4 hours)**:
   - Observe actual processes:
     * Patient screening dan consent procedures
     * Data collection dan documentation
     * Sample handling dan laboratory procedures
     * Investigational product management
     * Adverse event reporting systems
     * Quality control procedures

6. **Capability Testing (Day 2 - 2 hours)**:
   - Mock scenarios untuk critical processes:
     * Emergency response procedures
     * Data breach response
     * Protocol deviation handling
     * Investigational product recall
     * Serious adverse event reporting

7. **Closing Meeting (Day 3 - 2 hours)**:
   - Present preliminary findings
   - Discuss identified strengths dan weaknesses
   - Provide immediate feedback dan recommendations
   - Confirm timeline untuk final report
   - Answer questions dari CRU management

### 7.4 Data Analysis dan Scoring
**Waktu:** 3-5 hari kerja setelah on-site visit
1. **Assessment Team**:
   - Menggabungkan data dari desk assessment dan on-site visit
   - Melakukan detailed analysis untuk setiap domain
   - Menghitung weighted scores menggunakan Assessment Scoring Matrix
   - Menentukan overall maturity level:
     * Level 0-20: Not Ready
     * Level 21-40: Initial (Level 1)
     * Level 41-60: Developing (Level 2)
     * Level 61-80: Advanced (Level 3)
     * Level 81-100: Leading (Level 4)

   - Mengidentifikasi critical gaps dan improvement priorities:
     * High-impact, high-urgency gaps
     * High-impact, low-urgency gaps
     * Low-impact, high-urgency gaps
     * Low-impact, low-urgency gaps

### 7.5 Assessment Report Preparation
**Waktu:** 3-5 hari kerja setelah analysis
1. **Koordinator Asesmen**:
   - Menyusun Comprehensive Assessment Report (Lampiran E):
     * Executive summary dengan key findings
     * Detailed assessment results per domain
     * Overall maturity level determination
     * Strengths dan best practices identified
     * Critical gaps dan weaknesses
     * Specific recommendations untuk improvement
     * Development roadmap dengan timelines
     * Resource requirements untuk development

   - Quality assurance review:
     * Internal review oleh senior assessor
     * Fact-checking untuk accuracy
     * Consistency check dengan methodology
     * Compliance assessment dengan standards
     * Final approval oleh Kepala Tim Kerja

### 7.6 Result Communication dan Follow-Up
**Waktu:** 1-2 minggu setelah report completion
1. **Koordinator Asesmen**:
   - Scheduling result presentation meeting:
     * Present assessment findings ke CRU management
     * Discuss improvement recommendations
     * Answer questions dan provide clarification
     * Agreement pada development priorities
     * Establish timeline untuk implementation

   - Official notification:
     * Send official Assessment Result Letter (Lampiran F)
     * Include maturity level determination
     * Provide conditional approval status (jika applicable)
     * Specify requirements untuk full approval
     * Set re-assessment schedule

### 7.7 Development Planning dan Monitoring
**Waktu:** Ongoing dengan periodic review
1. **CRU Management**:
   - Develop Implementation Plan:
     * Prioritized improvement actions
     * Resource allocation dan budget planning
     * Timeline dengan specific milestones
     * Responsibility assignment untuk team members
     * Risk mitigation strategies

   - Implement improvement initiatives:
     * Facility upgrades atau new equipment
     * Staff recruitment atau training programs
     * SOP development atau quality system implementation
     * Process improvements dan standardization
     * Technology adoption atau system integration

2. **INA-CRC Monitoring Team**:
   - Regular monitoring dan support:
     * Quarterly progress reviews
     * Technical assistance untuk complex issues
     * Capacity building initiatives
     * Best practice sharing sessions
     * Access ke resources dan expertise

## 8. ASSESSMENT CRITERIA DAN SCORING

### 8.1 Infrastructure dan Facilities (Weight: 20%)
| Criteria | Score 0-20 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| Clinical Facilities | Physical facilities examination | Facility layout, equipment inventory |
| Laboratory Capability | Lab equipment dan methods validation | Lab certifications, validation reports |
| Investigational Product Storage | Proper storage conditions | Temperature monitoring, security systems |
| IT Infrastructure | System capability dan security | System documentation, security measures |

### 8.2 Human Resources (Weight: 25%)
| Criteria | Score 0-25 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| Principal Investigator Expertise | PI qualifications dan experience | CV, publication list, trial experience |
| Research Staff Competency | Staff qualifications dan training | Training records, competency assessments |
| Staff Availability | Sufficient staffing levels | Organizational chart, staffing plans |
| Training Programs | Regular training schedules | Training calendars, completion records |

### 8.3 Quality Systems (Weight: 20%)
| Criteria | Score 0-20 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| SOP Coverage | Comprehensive SOPs for all processes | SOP library, version control |
| Quality Management System | Formal quality system implementation | Quality manual, audit records |
* Document Control | Proper document management | Document control procedures |
* Continuous Improvement | Improvement programs effectiveness | Improvement project records |

### 8.4 Operational Capability (Weight: 15%)
| Criteria | Score 0-15 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| Patient Recruitment | Patient population dan recruitment capability | Patient data, recruitment strategies |
| Data Management | Data collection dan management systems | Data system documentation |
* Coordination Capability | Internal coordination mechanisms | Meeting records, communication logs |
* Financial Management | Sustainable financial planning | Financial statements, budget plans |

### 8.5 Regulatory Compliance (Weight: 15%)
| Criteria | Score 0-15 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
* Licensing dan Accreditation | Required licenses dan accreditations | License certificates, accreditation reports |
* GCP Compliance | Understanding dan application of GCP | Training records, compliance evidence |
* Ethical Compliance | Ethical review process compliance | Ethical approval records |
* Regulatory Reporting Proper reporting mechanisms | Reporting logs, communication records |

### 8.6 Financial Sustainability (Weight: 5%)
| Criteria | Score 0-5 | Evidence Required |
|----------|------------|------------------|
* Financial Planning | Comprehensive financial planning | Budget documents, financial projections |
* Revenue Streams | Diversified income sources | Revenue analysis, contracts |
* Cost Management | Efficient cost control measures | Cost analysis, efficiency reports |
* Sustainability Plan | Long-term sustainability strategy | Strategic plan, sustainability initiatives |

## 10. PEMANTAUAN DAN EVALUASI

### 10.1 Key Performance Indicators
- Assessment completion time: ≤ 4 minggu dari start hingga report
- Assessment quality score: ≥ 90% compliance dengan methodology
- CRU improvement rate: ≥ 80% dari assessed CRU show improvement
- Stakeholder satisfaction: ≥ 4.0 dari 5.0

### 10.2 Monitoring Schedule
- **Weekly**: Assessment progress tracking
- **Monthly**: Assessment quality reviews
- **Quarterly**: CRU development progress monitoring
- **Semi-annually**: Assessment methodology review
- **Annually**: Comprehensive assessment program evaluation

### 10.3 Evaluation Criteria
- Accuracy dan consistency dari assessment findings
- Effectiveness dari improvement recommendations
- CRU satisfaction dengan assessment process
- Impact dari assessment pada CRU development
- Quality dari assessment team dan methodology

## 11. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

### 11.1 Assessment Process Improvement
- Regular review dari assessment methodology

- Improve assessment tools dan templates

### 11.2 Best Practice Sharing
- Develop best practice library dari successful CRUs

## 12. RISK MANAGEMENT

### 12.1 Assessment Risks
- **Subjective Assessment**: Bias atau inconsistency dalam scoring
- **Incomplete Information**: Insufficient data untuk accurate assessment
- **Political Pressure**: External influence pada assessment outcomes
- **Resource Constraints**: Limited time atau resources untuk thorough assessment
- **Stakeholder Resistance**: Resistance dari CRU terhadap findings

### 12.2 Mitigation Strategies
- **Standardized Methodology**: Clear criteria dan consistent scoring
- **Multiple Assessors**: Team approach dengan diverse perspectives
- **Documentation Requirements**: Comprehensive evidence collection
- **Quality Assurance**: Independent review dari assessment findings
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Early involvement dan clear communication
