# SOP Pelaksanaan Audit dan Pendampingan Clinical Research Unit (CRU)
**ID:** INACRC-SOP-303
**Versi:** 1.0
**Tanggal Berlaku:** 1 Januari 2026
**Tanggal Tinjau:** 31 Desember 2026
**Disetujui oleh:** Kepala Tim Kerja INA-CRC

---

## 1. TUJUAN
Menyediakan kerangka kerja untuk melakukan audit internal secara berkala ke Clinical Research Unit (CRU) dan memberikan pendampingan perbaikan untuk memastikan kepatuhan terhadap Good Clinical Practice (GCP), regulasi, dan standar kualitas yang telah ditetapkan.

## 2. RUANG LINGKUP
SOP ini berlaku untuk:
- Tim Audit Internal INA-CRC
- Semua CRU yang tergabung dalam jejaring INA-CRC
- External Auditor (jika diperlukan untuk independent audit)
- Quality Assurance Team
- Management CRU dan rumah sakit
- Principal Investigator dan tim penelitian
- Sponsor dan regulatory authorities

## 3. DEFINISI
- **Audit**: Proses sistematis untuk mengevaluasi kepatuhan terhadap standar yang telah ditetapkan
- **Compliance**: Kepatuhan terhadap regulasi, GCP, dan prosedur internal
- **Finding**: Deviasi atau ketidaksesuaian yang diidentifikasi selama audit
- **Non-Conformance**: Ketidaksesuaian signifikan yang memerlukan corrective action
- **Corrective Action**: Tindakan untuk mengeliminasi penyebab non-conformance
- **Preventive Action**: Tindakan untuk mencegah terjadinya non-conformance
- **Mentoring**: Pendampingan untuk perbaikan berkelanjutan

## 4. REFERENSI
- ICH-GCP E6(R2) Guidelines Section 5.1
- KMK Nomor 1458 Tahun 2023 tentang Tata Kelola Uji Klinis
- ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System
- WHO Guidelines for GCP Compliance
- Best Practice Clinical Trial Auditing

## 5. TANGGUNG JAWAB

| Peran | Tanggung Jawab |
|-------|----------------|
| **Kepala Tim Kerja INA-CRC** | Menyetujui program audit, menyetujui audit findings, menetapkan improvement priorities |
| **Lead Auditor** | Memimpin audit, menyiapkan audit plan, melaksanakan audit, melaporkan findings |
| **Audit Team** | Melakukan audit activities, mengumpulkan evidence, mendokumentasikan findings |
| **CRU Management** | Memfasilitasi audit, menyediakan dokumen, implement corrective actions |
| **Quality Assurance** | Review audit methodology, validate findings, monitor corrective actions |
| **Mentor/Consultant** | Memberikan pendampingan untuk perbaikan, transfer knowledge |

## 6. JENIS AUDIT

### 6.1 Routine Internal Audit
**Frequency:** Annual untuk setiap CRU
**Scope:** Comprehensive evaluation dari semua GCP aspects
**Focus:**
- Compliance dengan SOPs dan working procedures
- Quality management system implementation
- Documentation completeness dan accuracy
- Staff competency dan training records
- Facility dan equipment maintenance
- Data integrity dan confidentiality

### 6.2 Focused Audit
**Frequency:** Sesuai kebutuhan (trigger-based)
**Scope:** Specific areas atau processes
**Triggers:**
- Significant protocol deviations
- Patient safety concerns
- Regulatory inspection findings
- Sponsor complaints atau concerns
- High risk trial activities

### 6.3 Pre-Regulatory Inspection Audit
**Frequency:** 2-3 bulan sebelum regulatory inspection
**Scope:** Full mock inspection
**Focus:**
- Readiness untuk regulatory inspection
- Common inspection findings preparation
- Staff interview preparation
- Documentation completeness
- Site presentation dan tour preparation

### 6.4 Follow-Up Audit
**Frequency:** 3-6 bulan setelah corrective action implementation
**Scope:** Verification dari corrective action effectiveness
**Focus:**
- Implementation verification dari corrective actions
- Sustained compliance assessment
- Root cause elimination verification
- System improvement evaluation

## 7. PROSEDUR AUDIT

### 7.1 Perencanaan Audit
**Waktu:** 4-6 minggu sebelum audit
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Annual audit planning:
     * Develop Annual Audit Schedule (Lampiran A)
     * Prioritize CRU berdasarkan risk assessment
     * Allocate resources dan audit team assignments
     * Coordinate dengan CRU management untuk scheduling
     * Define audit scope dan objectives

   - Specific audit preparation:
     * Develop Audit Plan untuk specific audit (Lampiran B)
     * Review CRU profile dan previous audit findings
     * Identify key processes dan high-risk areas
     * Prepare audit checklist dan templates
     * Assign audit team dengan appropriate expertise

### 7.2 Notifikasi dan Persiapan
**Waktu:** 2-4 minggu sebelum audit
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Send Audit Notification Letter (Lampiran C):
     * Audit dates, scope, dan objectives
     * Required documents dan preparations
     * Audit team composition
     * Expected access requirements
     * Pre-audit questionnaire (jika applicable)

2. **CRU Management**:
   - Preparation activities:
     * Complete pre-audit questionnaire
     * Organize required documents dan records
     * Schedule key personnel availability
     * Prepare facility tour routes
     * Notify relevant stakeholders tentang upcoming audit

### 7.3 Opening Meeting
**Waktu:** Hari pertama audit (2 jam)
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Opening agenda:
     * Introductions dari audit team dan CRU representatives
     * Audit objectives, scope, dan criteria explanation
     * Audit methodology dan process overview
     * Confidentiality commitment statement
     * Schedule confirmation dan logistics arrangement
     * Questions dan clarification session

2. **CRU Management**:
   - Opening presentation:
     * CRU overview dan recent developments
     * Current trials dan activities
     * Previous corrective action status
     * Known issues atau challenges
     * Resource availability untuk audit

### 7.4 Audit Execution
**Waktu:** 2-5 hari tergantung pada scope
1. **Document Review**:
   - **GCP Documentation**: SOPs, protocols, informed consents
   - **Quality Records**: Training records, calibration certificates, maintenance logs
   - **Trial Master Files**: Complete trial documentation sets
   - **Regulatory Documents**: Approvals, correspondences, safety reports
   - **Personnel Records**: CVs, training certificates, competency assessments

2. **Interviews**:
   - **Management Interview**: Leadership commitment, quality culture, resource allocation
   - **Principal Investigator Interview**: Protocol understanding, GCP application, patient safety
   - **Study Coordinator Interview**: Study management, documentation, coordination
   - **Staff Interviews**: Understanding of roles, SOP compliance, training effectiveness

3. **Observations**:
   - **Facility Tour**: Research facilities, laboratories, storage areas, emergency equipment
   - **Process Observation**: Informed consent process, data collection, sample handling
   - **System Review**: Data management systems, security measures, backup procedures
   - **Equipment Check**: Calibration status, maintenance records, proper usage

4. **Record Sampling**:
   - **Trial Records**: Subject recruitment, informed consent, source documentation
   - **Safety Records**: Adverse event reporting, follow-up documentation
   - **Quality Records**: SOP compliance, deviation reporting, corrective actions
   - **Training Records**: Staff competency, training effectiveness, gap analysis

### 7.5 Findings Documentation
**Waktu:** Selama audit execution
1. **Audit Team**:
   - Finding classification:
     * **Observation**: Minor non-conformance tanada significant impact
     * **Minor Finding**: Non-conformance dengan limited impact
     * **Major Finding**: Significant non-conformance dengan potential impact
     * **Critical Finding**: Serious non-conformance dengan immediate impact required

   - Documentation requirements:
     * Clear statement dari finding
     * Objective evidence supporting finding
     * Reference to applicable standard atau requirement
     * Assessment dari impact pada trial quality atau patient safety
     * Recommended corrective action (jika appropriate)

### 7.6 Closing Meeting
**Waktu:** Hari terakhir audit (2-3 jam)
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Closing agenda:
     * Summary dari audit activities dan coverage
     * Presentation dari findings (preliminary)
     * Discussion dari each finding dengan CRU management
     * Agreement pada fact base dari findings
     * Timeline untuk draft report delivery
     * Questions dan clarification session

2. **CRU Management**:
   - Response to findings:
     * Acceptance atau disagreement dengan findings
     * Additional context atau information
     * Initial corrective action planning
     * Resource commitment untuk improvements
     * Follow-up scheduling requests

### 7.7 Audit Report Preparation
**Waktu:** 1-2 minggu setelah audit
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Report structure (Lampiran D):
     * Executive summary dengan key findings
     * Audit scope, objectives, dan methodology
     * Detailed findings dengan evidence
     * Conformance assessment summary
     * Recommendations untuk improvement
     * Corrective action requirements dengan deadlines
     * Follow-up audit scheduling

   - Quality assurance review:
     * Internal review untuk accuracy dan completeness
     * Fact-checking semua findings
     * Consistency check dengan audit methodology
     * Compliance dengan reporting standards
     * Final approval oleh Kepala Tim Kerja

### 7.8 Report Distribution dan Follow-Up
**Waktu:** 1 minggu setelah report approval
1. **Lead Auditor**:
   - Report distribution:
     * Send final audit report ke CRU management
     * Copy to Kepala Tim Kerja INA-CRC
     * Summary report to relevant stakeholders (jika applicable)
     * Secure storage dalam audit archive

   - Follow-up coordination:
     * Schedule corrective action planning meeting
     * Define response timeline dan requirements
     * Establish communication protocols
     * Plan follow-up audit schedule

## 8. PENDAMPINGAN PERBAIKAN

### 8.1 Corrective Action Planning
**Waktu:** 1-2 minggu setelah report distribution
1. **CRU Management**:
   - Root cause analysis:
     * Investigate underlying causes untuk setiap finding
     * Use systematic tools (5 Whys, Fishbone Diagram, etc.)
     * Identify systemic issues versus individual problems
     * Assess recurrence probability

   - Action plan development:
     * Specific corrective actions untuk each finding
     * Action owners dengan clear responsibilities
     * Realistic timelines untuk implementation
     * Resource requirements dan allocation
     * Success criteria dan verification methods

2. **INA-CRC Mentor**:
   - Pendampingan support:
     * Review dan refine corrective action plans
     * Provide technical expertise dan guidance
     * Share best practices dari successful CRUs
     * Facilitate access ke resources atau training
     * Monitor progress dan provide ongoing support

### 8.2 Implementation Support
**Waktu:** Sesuai action plan timeline (typical 1-6 bulan)
1. **Mentor/Consultant**:
   - Implementation assistance:
     * Regular check-in meetings untuk progress monitoring
     * Problem-solving untuk implementation barriers
     * Training atau technical assistance as needed
     * Documentation guidance dan template development
     * Process optimization dan streamlining

2. **CRU Implementation Team**:
   - Execution activities:
     * Implement corrective actions per action plan
     * Document implementation progress
     * Train staff pada new processes atau procedures
     * Monitor effectiveness dari implemented changes
     * Report progress regularly ke INA-CRC

### 8.3 Effectiveness Verification
**Waktu:** 1-3 bulan setelah implementation completion
1. **Audit Team**:
   - Verification methods:
     * Document review dari implementation evidence
     * Interviews dengan staff untuk confirmation
     * Observation dari new processes in action
     * Data analysis untuk performance improvement
     * Compliance check dengan updated procedures

   - Effectiveness assessment:
     * Determine jika corrective actions effective
     * Assess jika root causes eliminated
     * Verify sustained compliance
     * Identify any new issues atau challenges
     * Document lessons learned

## 9. AUDIT CRITERIA DAN STANDARDS

### 9.1 GCP Compliance Criteria
- **Ethical Conduct**: Proper informed consent process dan patient protection
- **Protocol Compliance**: Adherence kepada approved protocol dan amendments
- **Documentation**: Complete, accurate, dan timely documentation
- **Safety Reporting**: Proper adverse event identification dan reporting
- **Data Integrity**: Accurate data collection dan management
- **Investigational Product**: Proper storage, handling, dan accountability

### 9.2 Quality System Criteria
- **SOP Implementation**: Effective implementation dari all relevant SOPs
- **Training Programs**: Adequate training dan competency assessment
- **Quality Control**: Effective quality control processes
- **Change Management**: Proper change control procedures
- **Document Control**: Effective document management system
- **Continuous Improvement**: Systematic improvement programs

### 9.3 Regulatory Compliance Criteria
- **Licensing dan Accreditation**: Valid licenses dan accreditations
- **Regulatory Approvals**: Proper regulatory approvals untuk trials
- **Reporting Requirements**: Timely regulatory reporting
- **Inspection Readiness**: Preparedness untuk regulatory inspections
- **Records Retention**: Proper retention dan archival dari records

## 10. RISK-BASED AUDIT APPROACH

### 10.1 Risk Assessment Factors
- **Trial Complexity**: Complexity level dari ongoing trials
- **Patient Population**: Vulnerability dari patient populations
- **Product Risk**: Risk level dari investigational products
- **Previous Findings**: History dari audit findings
- **Staff Turnover**: Rate dari staff changes
- **Compliance History**: Previous compliance performance

### 10.2 Risk-Based Scoring
| Risk Factor | Score 1-5 | Weight |
|-------------|------------|--------|
| Trial Complexity | 1-5 | 20% |
| Patient Vulnerability | 1-5 | 25% |
| Product Risk | 1-5 | 20% |
| Previous Findings | 1-5 | 15% |
| Staff Turnover | 1-5 | 10% |
| Compliance History | 1-5 | 10% |

### 10.3 Audit Frequency Based on Risk
- **High Risk (Score > 4.0)**: Audit setiap 6 bulan
- **Medium Risk (Score 2.5-4.0)**: Audit tahunan
- **Low Risk (Score < 2.5)**: Audit setiap 18 bulan

## 12. PEMANTAUAN DAN EVALUASI

### 12.1 Key Performance Indicators
- Audit completion rate: 100% sesuai schedule
- Finding closure rate: ≥ 90% dalam agreed timeline
- Recurrent finding rate: ≤ 20% dari previous findings
- CRU satisfaction: ≥ 4.0 dari 5.0
- Improvement effectiveness: ≥ 80% dari corrective actions effective

### 12.2 Monitoring Schedule
- **Per Audit**: Real-time monitoring, finding tracking
- **Monthly**: Audit progress review, corrective action status
- **Quarterly**: Audit program effectiveness review
- **Semi-annually**: Auditor competency assessment
- **Annually**: Comprehensive audit program evaluation

### 12.3 Evaluation Criteria
- Effectiveness dari audit methodology
- Quality dari audit findings dan recommendations
- Success dari corrective action implementation
- Improvement dalam CRU compliance performance
- Efficiency dari audit processes

## 13. CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

### 13.1 Audit Process Enhancement
- Regular review dari audit methodology
- Update audit criteria berdasarkan regulatory changes
- Improve audit tools dan technology
- Enhance auditor training dan competency
- Benchmark dengan industry best practices

### 13.2 Knowledge Sharing
- Develop best practice library dari successful improvements
- Organize knowledge sharing sessions antar CRUs
- Create case studies dari effective corrective actions
- Establish network untuk peer learning
- Share lessons learned dari audit findings

## 14. RISK MANAGEMENT

### 14.1 Audit Risks
- **Subjective Assessment**: Bias atau inconsistency dalam findings
- **Limited Scope**: Inadequate coverage dari critical areas
- **Poor Communication**: Misunderstanding dari findings atau requirements
- **Resource Constraints**: Insufficient time atau resources untuk thorough audit
- **Stakeholder Resistance**: Resistance terhadap findings atau recommendations

### 14.2 Mitigation Strategies
- **Standardized Methodology**: Clear criteria dan consistent processes
- **Comprehensive Planning**: Thorough risk-based audit planning
- **Clear Communication**: Regular communication dengan stakeholders
- **Adequate Resources**: Proper resource allocation dan scheduling
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Early involvement dan collaborative approach
