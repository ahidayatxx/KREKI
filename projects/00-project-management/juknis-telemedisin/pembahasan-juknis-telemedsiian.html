Pembaruan Petunjuk Teknis Telemedisin Indonesia

Roadmap Transformasi Digital Healthcare Menuju Universal Coverage 2030

⚖️ Kerangka Regulasi & Keamanan

  • Harmonisasi Regulasi: Matriks compliance 17+ regulasi dengan klausul supremasi
  • Data Sovereignty: Wajib server lokal, audit BSSN tahunan, Zero-Trust Architecture
  • Blockchain Layer: Audit trail immutable, consent management real-time
  • Integrasi SATUSEHAT: Mandatory 2027, interoperabilitas HL7 FHIR

🎓 SDM & Infrastruktur Adaptif

  • Sertifikasi 3-Tier: Basic, Advanced, RPL (Recognition Prior Learning)
  • Standar Regional: Urban (5 Mbps), Semi-urban (2 Mbps), 3T (512 Kbps store-forward)
  • Hub & Spoke Model: Equipment leasing subsidi 90%, mobile unit rotasi
  • 1000 Digital Champions: ToT masif, campaign literasi nasional

💰 Model Pembiayaan Value-Based

  • Bundled Payment: Flat rate + bonus outcome (HbA1c terkontrol, zero emergency)
  • Differential Pricing: Basic (Rp 0) - Premium (Rp 1 juta), transparansi upfront
  • Insentif 3T: 2x tarif, tax deduction 30%, DSO mandatory 4 jam/minggu
  • Blended Finance: Rp 500 M/tahun (APBN 40%, BPJS 30%, CSR 20%, Donor 10%)

🚀 Inovasi & Spesialisasi

  • Disease Matrix: Traffic light system (Green/Yellow/Red), update 6 bulan
  • 10 Specialty Guidelines: Derma, Kardio, Pedi, ObGyn, Psikiatri, dll.
  • Innovation Sandbox: 5 kota DHIZ, waiver 12-24 bulan, AI/IoMT pilot
  • Framework ASEAN: Second opinion lintas negara, reciprocal license
