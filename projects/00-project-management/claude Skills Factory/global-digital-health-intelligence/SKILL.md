# Global Digital Health Intelligence Skill

## Overview

This skill provides strategic intelligence on digital health developments worldwide, with enhanced coverage of Asia-Pacific region and Indonesia-specific developments. Perfect for healthcare executives, policy makers, digital health innovators, and investors seeking market intelligence across global and emerging markets.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need:
- Comprehensive analysis of recent digital health developments and trends
- Asia-Pacific regional digital health ecosystem updates
- Indonesia-specific digital health policy, regulation, and market developments
- Global digital health investment and partnership announcements
- Technology adoption patterns and digital transformation insights
- Competitive intelligence and market sizing analysis
- Regulatory changes affecting digital health implementations
