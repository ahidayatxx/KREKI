# Indonesia Business Outlook - December 2024

## Concise Executive Summary

Indonesia's economy demonstrates resilience with 3.2% annual growth, driven by strong domestic consumption and improved export performance. Key sectors showing momentum include digital infrastructure, renewable energy, and manufacturing, while traditional retail faces headwinds from global competition. Inflation management remains effective within target range, and monetary policy supports continued investment attractiveness.

## Key Metrics

- **GDP Growth**: 3.2% annual expansion
- **Inflation**: 2.8% (within 2.5% target band)
- **Investment**: USD 42 billion FDI committed
- **Currency Stability**: Rupiah maintained 15,500-15,800 range vs USD

## Sectoral Highlights

**Technology & Digital**: 5G rollout accelerating digital economy
**Manufacturing**: 8.5% growth in export-oriented production
**Infrastructure**: Port development and renewable energy expansion
**Financial Services**: FinTech adoption and digital banking modernization

## Risk Factors

**Supply Chain**: Global trade tensions affecting export markets
**Climate**: Weather patterns impacting agricultural output
**Policy**: Regulatory transition in mining sector
**Competition**: ASEAN market integration pressures

## Strategic Implications

**Positive**: Economic fundamentals support continued investment
**Neutral**: Policy balance between growth and stability
**Challenges**: Technology adoption and skills gap in workforce

## 12-Month Outlook

First quarter 2025 expected to maintain growth trajectory with policy continuity supporting investor confidence and business expansion opportunities.
