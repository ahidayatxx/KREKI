# Indonesia News Briefing Skill

## Overview

This skill provides professional, concise news briefings about Indonesia's most important developments from the country's top national news sources. Perfect for business executives, policymakers, and professionals who need to stay informed about current events affecting Indonesia's business and policy landscape.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need:
- Daily executive briefings on Indonesian current events
- Business impact analysis of major news developments
- Policy and regulatory changes affecting business operations
- Economic indicators and market updates
- Political developments with business implications

## Instructions for Claude

When this skill is activated, follow this workflow:

### 1. Date Identification
First identify today's date using system date or asking if unsure.

### 2. Multi-Source News Gathering
Use a two-tier approach for comprehensive coverage:

**General Overview (Brave Search):**
- Search for: "Indonesia news today [current date]"
- Focus on: Top headlines, major developments, breaking news
- Priority: Broad coverage of significant events

**Specific Details (Tavily Search):**
- Source 1: Kompas.com - Search for "Kompas Indonesia [current date] headlines"
- Source 2: Republika.co.id - Search for "Republika Indonesia [current date] headlines"
- Source 3: Detik.com - Search for "Detik Indonesia [current date] headlines"
- Source 4: Antara News - Search for "Antara News Indonesia [current date] headlines"
- Source 5: Tempo.co - Search for "Tempo Indonesia [current date] headlines"

### 3. Content Selection and Analysis
From gathered news, select and analyze:

**Selection Criteria:**
- Top 3-5 most important stories from each source
- Events with significant business/economic impact
- Policy changes affecting industry or investment
- Major infrastructure or regulatory developments
- International trade or diplomatic news involving Indonesia

**Analysis Focus:**
- **Executive Summary**: Brief 2-3 sentence overview
- **Business Impact**: How news affects Indonesian businesses
- **Policy Implications**: Regulatory or government policy changes
- **Economic Significance**: Market effects, investment climate
- **Professional Context**: Background for informed decision-making
