# AMANA Advisory Board - Terms of Reference

**Document Type:** Official Terms of Reference
**Date:** 2025
**Subject:** Terms of Reference for Member of AMANA Advisory Board
**Classification:** Internal Reference

---

## 1. Purpose

The establishment of the AMANA Advisory Board aims to provide high-level strategic guidance, oversight, and expertise to support AMANA's mission in improving public goods delivery, particularly within the health sector. The Advisory Board will ensure that AMANA's initiatives are aligned with best practices, evidence-based approaches, and the evolving needs of the communities served.

---

## 2. Specific Role: Dr. Ahmad Hidayat, MBA

Dr. Ahmad Hidayat, MBA, is appointed as a Member of the AMANA Advisory Board. His specific responsibilities include:

### 2.1 Strategic Health Policy & Governance Advisory

- **Policy Development:** Provide expert advice on the development and implementation of strategic health policies that align with AMANA's mission and objectives.
- **Governance Frameworks:** Assist in establishing robust governance frameworks to ensure transparency, accountability, and effectiveness in AMANA's operations.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Offer guidance on navigating complex regulatory environments and ensuring compliance with relevant health sector regulations.

### 2.2 ASEAN Health Sector Engagement & Partnership Development

- **Regional Collaboration:** Leverage expertise and networks within the ASEAN health sector to foster partnerships and collaborations that enhance AMANA's regional presence and impact.
- **Stakeholder Engagement:** Identify and engage key stakeholders, including government agencies, non-governmental organizations, and private sector entities, to build coalitions for public goods improvement.
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Facilitate the exchange of knowledge and best practices across ASEAN countries to inform AMANA's strategies and initiatives.

### 2.3 Leadership & Organizational Development Coaching

- **Capacity Building:** Provide coaching and mentorship to AMANA's leadership team to strengthen their strategic thinking, decision-making, and leadership capabilities.
- **Organizational Development:** Advise on organizational structure, culture, and processes to enhance AMANA's effectiveness and sustainability.
- **Talent Development:** Support the development of emerging leaders within AMANA through targeted training and development programs.

### 2.4 Thought Leadership & Communication Support

- **Content Creation:** Contribute to the development of thought leadership content, including articles, presentations, and policy briefs, to position AMANA as a leader in public goods delivery innovation.
- **Public Speaking:** Represent AMANA at conferences, seminars, and other public forums to advocate for the organization's mission and share insights on health sector transformation.
- **Media Engagement:** Provide expert commentary and analysis on health sector issues to relevant media outlets, enhancing AMANA's visibility and credibility.

---

## 3. SEED Values Alignment

The Advisory Board Member's contributions will be guided by AMANA's SEED values:

### 3.1 Skin-in-the-Game (S)

- Demonstrate genuine commitment and accountability by actively participating in Board meetings and related activities.
- Take ownership of assigned responsibilities and follow through on commitments to AMANA.

### 3.2 Excellence (E)

- Uphold the highest standards of professional conduct and expertise in all advisory functions.
- Continuously seek to improve the quality and impact of advice provided to AMANA.

### 3.3 Empathy (E)

- Approach all advisory work with a deep understanding of and compassion for the communities AMANA serves.
- Ensure that policies and strategies are people-centered and address real needs.

### 3.4 Diversity (D)

- Value and promote diverse perspectives within the Advisory Board and across AMANA's initiatives.
- Ensure that strategies consider the needs of diverse populations, particularly marginalized and vulnerable groups.

---

## 4. Institutional Support and Interaction

### 4.1 Reporting Structure

The Advisory Board Member will report to and collaborate with:

- **AMANA Executive Director/CEO:** For day-to-day coordination and strategic alignment.
- **AMANA Board of Directors:** For high-level governance and oversight decisions.
- **Relevant AMANA Departments:** For specific advisory support and partnership development.

### 4.2 Meetings and Communication

- **Board Meetings:** Attend quarterly Advisory Board meetings to provide strategic input and review progress.
- **Ad-Hoc Consultations:** Be available for ad-hoc consultations as needed, either virtually or in-person, to address urgent strategic issues.
- **Communication Channels:** Maintain regular communication through email, phone, or virtual platforms to ensure timely advisory support.

### 4.3 Access to Information

- AMANA will provide the Advisory Board Member with access to relevant organizational documents, strategic plans, and performance reports necessary for informed advisory contributions.
- The Advisory Board Member will adhere to confidentiality agreements and data protection policies regarding sensitive information.

---

## 5. Exclusions

### 5.1 Operational Management

The Advisory Board Member's role is strictly advisory and does not include:

- Direct operational management or decision-making authority over AMANA's day-to-day activities.
- Personnel management responsibilities, including hiring, firing, or direct supervision of staff.
- Financial management or fiduciary responsibilities beyond advisory input on budget allocations.

### 5.2 Conflicts of Interest

- The Advisory Board Member must disclose any potential conflicts of interest and recuse themselves from related discussions if necessary.
- The role does not authorize the member to represent AMANA in contractual negotiations or bind the organization in any legal or financial commitments without explicit authorization.

### 5.3 Public Representation

- While the Advisory Board Member may represent AMANA at public forums as part of thought leadership activities, such representation must be coordinated with and approved by AMANA's leadership.
- The member does not have the authority to make public statements on behalf of AMANA without prior approval.

---

## 6. Time Commitment and Compensation

### 6.1 Expected Time Commitment

The expected time commitment for the Advisory Board Member role is approximately:

- **8-10 hours per month** on average, including:
  - Quarterly Board meetings (4 hours each)
  - Preparation and follow-up for meetings (2-3 hours per quarter)
  - Ad-hoc consultations and advisory support (2-3 hours per month)

### 6.2 Compensation

- **Honorarium:** A modest honorarium may be provided to recognize the time and expertise contributed, subject to AMANA's budget and policies.
- **Reimbursement:** Reasonable expenses incurred in the performance of advisory duties (e.g., travel to meetings) will be reimbursed in accordance with AMANA's expense policy.

### 6.3 Term of Appointment

- The initial term of appointment is **two (2) years**, with the possibility of renewal upon mutual agreement.
- Either party may terminate the arrangement with **30 days' written notice**.

---

## 7. Key Performance Indicators

The effectiveness of the Advisory Board Member's contributions will be assessed based on:

### 7.1 Strategic Impact

- Quality and relevance of strategic advice provided to AMANA.
- Influence on AMANA's strategic direction and key decisions.

### 7.2 Partnership Development

- Number and quality of partnerships established within the ASEAN health sector.
- Success in leveraging networks for AMANA's benefit.

### 7.3 Capacity Building

- Improvement in the leadership and organizational capabilities of AMANA's team.
- Feedback from AMANA staff on the value of coaching and mentorship received.

### 7.4 Thought Leadership

- Number and impact of thought leadership contributions (articles, presentations, media engagements).
- Enhancement of AMANA's reputation and visibility in the health sector.

### 7.5 Values Alignment

- Demonstration of SEED values in all advisory interactions.
- Integration of SEED values into AMANA's strategies and initiatives.

---

## 8. Legal and Ethical Considerations

### 8.1 Confidentiality

- The Advisory Board Member must maintain strict confidentiality regarding all sensitive information obtained during the course of their advisory duties.
- Confidentiality obligations extend beyond the term of appointment.

### 8.2 Ethical Conduct

- The Advisory Board Member must adhere to the highest ethical standards, avoiding any actions that could harm AMANA's reputation or interests.
- Any breach of ethical standards may result in immediate termination of the advisory arrangement.

### 8.3 Liability

- AMANA will maintain appropriate insurance coverage to protect the Advisory Board Member from liability arising from the performance of their advisory duties, provided the member acts in good faith and within the scope of their role.

---

## 9. Amendment and Review

### 9.1 Amendment

These Terms of Reference may be amended by mutual written agreement between AMANA and the Advisory Board Member.

### 9.2 Review

These Terms of Reference will be reviewed annually to ensure they remain relevant and effective in guiding the Advisory Board Member's contributions.
