## Template Usage Notes

### When to Use This Template

- Articles for industry publications
- Op-eds for newspapers or online platforms
- LinkedIn thought leadership posts
- Conference speaking content
- Strategic meeting talking points
- Webinar or presentation content

### Key Quality Indicators

- Compelling hook that grabs attention in first 10 seconds
- Clear progression from issue to insights to action
- Evidence-based with accessible language
- Specific, actionable recommendations
- ASEAN/Indonesia context included where relevant
- Human impact and beneficiary focus evident
- Author bio establishes credibility

### Length Guidelines

- **Full Article:** 1,200-1,500 words
- **Op-Ed:** 600-1,000 words
- **LinkedIn Post:** 300-500 words
- **Conference Presentation:** 15-20 minutes speaking time (~1,500-2,000 words)

### Customization by Format

**For Publications:**
- Include publication-specific formatting
- Adjust word count to venue requirements
- Include publication-ready imagery suggestions

**For LinkedIn:**
- Shorter paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- More subheadings for scannability
- Engagement questions throughout
- Hashtag strategy (3-5 relevant tags)

**For Speaking:**
- Include speaker notes and timing
- Add visual/slide descriptions
- Mark emphasis and pause points
- Include audience interaction prompts
