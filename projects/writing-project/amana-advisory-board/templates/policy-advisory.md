## Template Usage Notes

### When to Use This Template

- Policy briefs for decision-makers
- Position papers on health sector issues
- Advocacy materials for policy reform
- Legislative testimony preparation
- Regulatory commentaries
- Policy analysis for stakeholders

### Key Quality Indicators

- Executive summary under 300 words
- Every claim cited with APA 7th reference
- Policy options fairly presented
- Implementation pathway realistic
- Stakeholder impacts addressed
- Equity considerations prominent
- Recommendations specific and actionable
- SEED values evident

### Length Guidelines

- **Executive Policy Brief:** 1,500-2,000 words
- **Detailed Position Paper:** 2,500-4,000 words
- **Advocacy Summary:** 800-1,200 words
- **Testimony:** 1,000-1,500 words (5-7 minutes speaking)

### Customization by Policy Type

**Regulatory Policy:** Add compliance section, industry considerations

**Funding Policy:** Add cost-benefit analysis, financing options

**Governance Policy:** Add institutional roles, accountability mechanisms

**Digital Health Policy:** Add technical feasibility, privacy/security

**Public Health Policy:** Add health impact assessment prominently
