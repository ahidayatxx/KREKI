## Template Usage Notes

### When to Use This Template

- Strategic advisory for AMANA leadership decisions
- Policy analysis on health sector developments
- Governance framework recommendations
- Decision support for major initiatives
- Assessment of strategic options

### Key Quality Indicators

- Executive summary under 300 words
- Clear progression from context to recommendations
- Options fairly presented with pros/cons
- Recommendations actionable and prioritized
- All claims cited in APA 7th format
- SEED values evident in analysis

### Customization Options

- Add/remove sections based on specific needs
- Expand options assessment for complex decisions
- Include visual frameworks or diagrams where helpful
- Adjust length based on decision urgency and complexity
