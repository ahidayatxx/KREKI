## Template Usage Notes

### When to Use This Template

- Regular board or leadership updates
- Progress reports on initiatives
- Partnership communications
- Stakeholder briefings on developments
- Meeting summaries and action items
- Introduction and networking messages

### Key Quality Indicators

- Clear subject line that indicates content
- Information organized by priority
- Strategic highlights identify most important points
- Balanced presentation of successes and challenges
- Specific next steps with owners and timelines
- Professional and relationship-building tone
- Appropriate level of detail for audience

### Customization by Audience

**For AMANA Leadership (ICP-01):**
- Highest discretion with both successes and challenges
- Strategic focus with operational implications
- Comprehensive but concise
- Direct and action-oriented

**For ASEAN Policymakers (ICP-02):**
- Diplomatic and respectful tone
- Regional context included
- Partnership opportunities highlighted
- Formal with approachability

**For Partners (ICP-03):**
- Collaborative tone
- Mutual benefit emphasized
- Clear about roles and expectations
- Relationship-building focus

**For Internal Team (ICP-04):**
- Informative and developmental
- Clear expectations and support
- Growth-oriented language
- Accessible and approachable

### Length Guidelines

- **Executive Update:** 1-2 pages (400-600 words)
- **Comprehensive Report:** 3-5 pages (800-1,200 words)
- **Quick Update:** 0.5-1 page (200-300 words)
- **Meeting Summary:** 1-2 pages (400-600 words)
