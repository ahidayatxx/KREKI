## Struktur & Delivery Training Programs

### Modul Training Komprehensif

Ahmad mendesain training architecture yang scalable dan modular:

**Foundation Level - GCP Fundamentals & Regulatory Framework**:
- ICH-GCP principles interpretation dengan Indonesian regulatory context
- BPOM requirements untuk clinical trial authorization dan ethical clearance
- INA-CRC coordination mechanisms dan national clinical research infrastructure
- Informed consent process dalam multi-literacy populations
- Essential documents management dan regulatory file preparation

**Intermediate Level - Role-Specific Operational Excellence**:
- Study Coordinator Track: Source documentation mastery, query management, patient retention strategies
- Investigator Track: Protocol adherence, medical decision authority, safety reporting obligations  
- CRA Track: Monitoring visit conduct, SDV techniques, CAPA implementation
- Regulatory Affairs Track: Submission preparation, communication dengan BPOM, amendment management

**Advanced Level - Strategic Clinical Research Leadership**:
- Quality risk management dan risk-based monitoring implementation
- Data integrity frameworks dan fraud detection systems
- Investigator-initiated trials (IIT) management
- Global clinical trial operations dalam multi-country studies
- Regulatory inspection preparedness dan remediation planning

### Simulation-Based Learning Components

Terinspirasi dari Pro-ficiency model simulation-based training yang Ahmad profil untuk IDI evaluations, ia mengintegrasikan:

**Protocol-Specific Workshops**: Peserta assigned therapeutic area-specific protocols (oncology, infectious diseases, cardiovascular) dan menjalankan full trial lifecycle simulation—dari site selection feasibility hingga database lock dan study report finalization.

**Mock Monitoring Visits**: CRA trainees conduct site monitoring pada simulated eCRF datasets yang Ahmad populate dengan realistic data patterns—including intentional protocol deviations, missing data, dan consistency issues that require investigation.

**Adverse Event Management Drills**: Real-time simulation dimana investigators must assess causality, determine severity grading, dan execute reporting timelines untuk SAEs—dengan Ahmad role-playing sebagai sponsor medical monitor yang queries their medical judgments.

**Regulatory Authority Interactions**: Mock scenarios simulasi BPOM inspection atau INA-CRC assessment visit, melatih peserta dalam professional communication, documentation presentation, dan regulatory inquiry responses.

- Pre-training asynchronous modules via learning management system (LMS) untuk foundational concepts

- Post-training virtual office hours untuk ongoing mentorship dan case consultations
- Alumni network untuk continuous professional development dan knowledge exchange

**Institutional Partnerships**: Leveraging Ahmad's network across pharmaceutical companies, CROs, investigator sites, dan academic medical centers, training programs incorporate:
