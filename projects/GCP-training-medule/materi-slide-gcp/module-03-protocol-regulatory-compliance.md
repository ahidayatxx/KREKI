# Slide 3: ET/KE Submission - Overview

## **Komite Etik Penelitian Review Process**

### **Jenis ET/KE di Indonesia:**
- **ET Lokal:** Institutional review boards
- **ET Pusat:** National ethics committees
- **Komisi Etik Nasional:** National oversight body
- **KE Khusus:** Disease-specific committees

### **Persyaratan Submission:**
- **Protokol:** Complete study plan
- **Investigator Brochure:** Product information
- **Persetujuan Setelah Penjelasan:** Subject information
- **Investigator CV:** Qualifications documentation
- **Informasi Fasilitas:** Site capabilities

### **Review Timeline:**
- **Tinjauan Awal:** 30-60 hari
- **Tinjauan Dipercepat:** 7-14 hari
- **Tinjauan Berkelanjutan:** Tahunan
- **Tinjauan Amendemen:** 14-30 hari

> ⚠️ **Penting:** ET approval diperlukan sebelum PPUK submission

---
**Rujukan:** Pedoman KEPK RI; BPOM No. 8 Tahun 2024

# Slide 5: ET/KE Review Process

## **Kategori dan Prosedur Tinjauan**

### **Tinjauan Penuh Board:**
- **Kriteria:** Greater than minimal risk studies
- **Proses:** Complete board discussion
- **Timeline:** 30-60 hari
- **Keputusan:** Disetujui, minor/major revisi, ditunda, ditolak

### **Tinjauan Dipercepat:**
- **Kriteria:** Minimal risk studies, amendments
- **Proses:** Single reviewer or chair
- **Timeline:** 7-14 hari
- **Contoh:** Minimal risk drug studies, record reviews

### **Tinjauan Berkelanjutan:**
- **Frekuensi:** Annual minimum
- **Dokumen:** Progress report, safety update
- **Proses:** Similar to initial review
- **Timeline:** 30-45 hari sebelum expiry

### **Pertimbangan Khusus:**
- **Populasi Rentan:** Additional requirements
- **International Studies:** Local context adaptation
- **Situasi Darurat:** Expedited procedures

---
**Rujukan:** Kategori Tinjauan KEPK; ICH E6(R3) 3.1-3.3
<!-- CATATAN PEMBICARA: Understanding review categories helps planning timeline dan resource allocation. Expedited review dapat significantly speed up process untuk eligible studies. -->

# Slide 7: Penyimpangan Protokol - Overview

## **Jenis dan Klasifikasi**

### **Definisi Penyimpangan Protokol:**
Any departure from the study protocol that is not a protocol violation

### **Sistem Klasifikasi:**

| Kategori | Tingkat Keparahan | Timeline | Contoh |
|----------|-------------------|----------|--------|
| **Minor** | Low impact | Rutin reporting | Visit window ± 3 hari |
| **Major** | Potential impact | 7-24 jam | Dose omission |
| **Critical** | Serious impact | Segera | Safety protocol breach |
| **Violation** | Intentional | 24 jam | Unauthorized changes |

### **Common Deviation Types:**
- **Timing:** Visit windows, dosing schedules
- **Procedures:** Missed procedures, extra tests
- **Eligibility:** Ineligible subject enrollment
- **Dosing:** Incorrect dose or timing
- **Safety:** Missed safety assessments

---
**Rujukan:** ICH E6(R3) 4.12; Panduan Manajemen Deviasi
<!-- CATATAN PEMBICARA: Proper deviation classification adalah essential untuk appropriate response dan reporting. Not all deviations require urgent reporting. -->

# Slide 8: Manajemen Penyimpangan Protokol

## **Kerangka Kerja Manajemen**

### **Tindakan Segera:**
1. **Identifikasi:** Recognize deviation occurrence
2. **Assess:** Evaluate impact dan severity
3. **Document:** Complete deviation form
4. **Report:** Notify appropriate parties
5. **Correct:** Implement corrective actions

### **Persyaratan Dokumentasi:**
- **Form Deviasi:** Detailed description
- **Impact Assessment:** Subject safety, data integrity
- **Corrective Actions:** Prevention measures
- **Root Cause Analysis:** Why deviation occurred
- **Follow-up:** Resolution confirmation

### **Persyaratan Pelaporan:**
| Jenis Deviasi | Ke Sponsor | Ke ET | Ke BPOM |
|----------------|------------|-------|---------|
| Minor | Bulanan | Tahunan | Tidak |
| Major | Dalam 7 hari | Dalam 15 hari | Kasus per kasus |
| Critical | Dalam 24 jam | Dalam 7 hari | Diperlukan |
| Violation | Dalam 24 jam | Dalam 7 hari | Diperlukan |

---
**Rujukan:** SOP Manajemen Deviasi; Persyaratan Pelaporan BPOM

# Slide 10: PPUK Process - Overview

## **Persetujuan Pelaksanaan Uji Klinik**

### **Definisi PPUK:**
Official approval from BPOM for conducting clinical trials in Indonesia

### **Framework Regulasi:**
- **Regulasi Primer:** BPOM No. 8 Tahun 2024
- **Supporting Regulations:** Guidelines untuk specific products
- **Standar Internasional:** ICH, WHO guidelines
- **ASEAN Harmonization:** Regional alignment

### **Kategori Submission:**
- **Drug Applications:** First-in-human studies
- **Existing Drug Extensions:** New indications
- **Bioequivalence Studies:** Generic drug approval
- **Medical Device Studies:** Device safety/efficacy
- **Herbal Medicine:** Traditional product validation

### **Processing Timeline:**
- **Validasi:** 5-10 hari kerja
- **Review:** 60-90 hari kerja
- **Inspection:** Case-by-case
- **Approval:** 5-10 hari kerja

> ⏱️ **Total:** 70-110 hari kerja (typical)

---
**Rujukan:** BPOM No. 8 Tahun 2024; Pedoman PPUK
<!-- CATATAN PEMBICARA: PPUK process adalah unique Indonesian system. Understanding timeline dan requirements critical untuk study planning. -->

# Slide 12: PPUK Review Process

## **Tinjauan Stages dan Kriteria**

### **Stage 1: Administrative Review (5-10 hari)**
- **Document Completeness:** All required documents
- **Format Compliance:** Proper formatting
- **Fee Payment:** Processing fee confirmation
- **Eligibility:** Meets submission criteria

### **Stage 2: Scientific Review (30-45 hari)**
- **Protocol Quality:** Study design appropriateness
- **Risk-Benefit Assessment:** Subject protection
- **Investigator Qualifications:** Competency verification
- **Facility Capability:** Site readiness

### **Stage 3: Technical Review (15-30 hari)**
- **Product Quality:** Manufacturing standards
- **Stability Data:** Product stability
- **Labeling Compliance:** Indonesian requirements
- **Import Documentation:** Customs procedures

### **Stage 4: Final Review (5-10 hari)**
- **Overall Assessment:** Complete evaluation
- **Conditions of Approval:** Special requirements
- **Inspection Planning:** Site inspection needs
- **Approval Decision:** Final determination

# Slide 13: PPUK for Multi-center Trials

## **Pertimbangan Khusus**

### **Single Ethical Assessment:**
- **Primary ET:** Primary investigator's institution conducts main review
- **Local ETs:** Administrative review at participating sites
- **Coordination:** Centralized communication
- **Documentation:** All ET approvals coordinated

### **Site Requirements:**
- **Primary Site:** PI institution with primary ET
- **Participating Sites:** Local approval requirements
- **Site Qualification:** GCP compliance verification
- **Infrastructure:** Standardized systems

### **BPOM Considerations:**
- **Single Application:** One PPUK for all sites
- **Site List:** Complete site documentation
- **Investigator Documentation:** All site investigators
- **Feasibility:** Site capability assessment

### **Implementation Process:**
1. **Primary ET Approval:** Initial ethical review
2. **Site Selection:** Qualified participating sites
3. **Local Approvals:** Administrative site approvals
4. **PPUK Submission:** Complete multi-center application
5. **Site Activation:** Sequential or parallel

---
**Rujukan:** Pedoman Uji Coba Multi-center; Koordinasi ET SOP

# Slide 15: Protocol Amendments - Overview

## **Jenis dan Kategori**

### **Definisi Amendment:**
Any change to the study protocol after initial approval

### **Kategori Amendments:**

| Jenis | Tingkat | Review Required | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------------|----------|
| **Administratif** | Low | Administrative | 5-10 hari |
| **Minor** | Moderate | Limited review | 10-15 hari |
| **Major** | High | Full review | 20-30 hari |
| **Safety** | Critical | Urgent review | 3-7 hari |

### **Common Amendment Types:**
- **Administratif:** Investigator changes, contact updates
- **Prosedural:** Visit scheduling changes
- **Scientific:** Endpoint modifications, sample size changes
- **Safety:** New safety information, protocol changes
- **Regulatory:** New requirements, compliance updates

### **Special Situations:**
- **Urgent Amendments:** Safety-related changes
- **Substantial Amendments:** Major protocol modifications
- **Administrative Amendments:** Minor updates

---
**Rujukan:** ICH E6(R3) 6.4; SOP Protocol Amendments
<!-- CATATAN PEMBICARA: Amendment management adalah ongoing process selama study. Proper classification memastikan appropriate review timeline. -->

# Slide 16: Amendment Management Process

## **Prosedur Manajemen Amendemen**

### **Step 1: Amendment Identification**
- **Need Assessment:** Justifikasi perubahan
- **Impact Analysis:** Effect pada study conduct
- **Regulatory Review:** Compliance requirements
- **Stakeholder Input:** Team dan sponsor consultation

### **Step 2: Documentation Preparation**
- **Amendment Document:** Detailed change description
- **Rationale Document:** Justification untuk perubahan
- **Impact Assessment:** Subject safety, data integrity
- **Updated Documents:** All affected documents

### **Step 3: Review and Approval**
- **Internal Review:** Study team evaluation
- **ET Review:** Ethics committee approval
- **BPOM Review:** Regulatory approval (if required)
- **Site Implementation:** Local communication

### **Step 4: Implementation**
- **Site Training:** Staff education
- **Document Updates:** All affected materials
- **Subject Communication:** Informed consent updates
- **System Changes:** eCRF, tracking systems
