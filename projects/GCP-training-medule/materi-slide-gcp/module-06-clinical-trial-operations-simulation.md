# 🎭 **Simulasi Modul 6: Operasional Uji Klinis**
## **Revolusi Operasional Era Digital: IoT, Real-Time Monitoring, dan Multi-Site Intelligence**

---

## 📋 **Gambaran Umum Simulasi**

### **Konteks:**
Anda adalah **Operations Manager untuk multi-site adaptive platform trial** pertama di Indonesia dengan **IoT-enabled investigational product management**, **real-time quality monitoring systems**, dan **AI-powered compliance automation**. Penelitian ini berada di 10 situs dengan 6 provinsi berbeda dan menggunakan **smart supply chain technology** yang belum pernah ada di Indonesia.

### **Inovasi Operasional Digital:**
- **IoT-enabled storage monitoring** dengan real-time temperature, humidity, and location tracking
- **AI-powered quality assurance** dengan automated deviation detection and predictive failure analysis
- **Blockchain documentation** dengan immutable audit trails untuk regulatory compliance
- **Smart dispensing systems** dengan biometric verification and automated documentation
- **Drone delivery network** untuk investigational product transport ke remote sites

### **Detail Penelitian:**
- **Indikasi:** Multi-indication oncology platform dengan adaptive protocol
- **Intervensi:** Personalized immunotherapy dengan AI-driven patient matching
- **Duration:** 36 bulan dengan rolling enrollment dan adaptive design
- **Participants:** 500 pasien di 10 situs (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Medan, Makassar, Denpasar, Palembang, Semarang, Yogyakarta, Manado)
- **Technology Platform:** Comprehensive digital operations ecosystem dengan nationwide IoT network

## 🤔 **Critical Decision Framework**

### **Operations Crisis Response Options:**

#### **Option A: Full Manual Operations Override**
- **Activate Manual Procedures:** Complete switch to paper-based systems
- **Emergency Resource Deployment:** Rapid deployment of manual supplies and staff
- **Regulatory Emergency Protocols:** Special authorization for manual compliance
- **Patient Safety Priority:** Immediate focus on critical patient needs
- **Pro:** Maximum patient safety focus, maintains study continuity
- **Contra:** Significant regulatory compliance burden, high resource requirements

#### **Option B: Hybrid Digital-Analog Approach**
- **Critical Digital Systems:** Maintain digital for patient safety-critical functions
- **Manual Backup:** Use manual systems for non-critical administrative functions
- **Prioritized Resource Allocation:** Focus technology resources on high-priority sites
- **Phased System Recovery:** Gradual restoration of digital capabilities
- **Pro:** Balanced approach, resource optimization
- **Contra:** Complex coordination requirements, partial functionality limitations

#### **Option C: Temporary Study Pause**
- **Immediate Study Hold:** Pause all non-emergency study activities
- **System Recovery Focus:** Prioritize technology system restoration
- **Patient Safety Maintenance:** Continue essential patient care outside study context
- **Regulatory Notification:** Formal study hold notification to BPOM
- **Pro:** Allows complete system recovery, minimizes compliance risks
- **Contra:** Significant study delays, potential patient withdrawal risks

### **Key Decision Questions:**

1. **Patient Safety vs. Study Integrity:**
   - How to balance immediate patient needs with long-term study integrity?
   - When does manual procedure risk become unacceptable?
   - How to prioritize limited emergency resources across multiple sites?

2. **Technology Reliability vs. Human Intervention:**
   - Which digital systems are essential versus dispensable?
   - How to validate manual procedures for regulatory compliance?
   - When should technology be abandoned entirely for manual methods?

3. **Regulatory Compliance vs. Emergency Response:**
   - How to document emergency protocol deviations appropriately?
   - What regulatory notifications are required immediately?
   - How to maintain audit trail integrity during system failures?

4. **Multi-Site Coordination vs. Local Autonomy:**
   - How much decision-making authority should local sites have?
   - When should centralized control override local decisions?
   - How to maintain consistency while allowing site-specific adaptations?

### **Digital Operations Transformation:**
1. **Technology redundancy is essential** - Single points of failure unacceptable in critical operations
2. **Human judgment must override automated systems** during emergency situations
3. **Manual procedure competence remains critical** despite technological advances
4. **Cross-functional coordination** becomes more complex in distributed digital operations

### **Multi-Site Operational Excellence:**
1. **Centralized coordination with local autonomy** provides optimal balance for crisis response
2. **Standardized procedures with site-specific adaptations** maintain consistency while allowing flexibility

4. **Resource allocation algorithms** must consider both efficiency and equity
