VaxiConnect System Architecture

An integrated WhatsApp system for automated immunization scheduling and tracking.

PPresentation Layer
WhatsApp Interface
Handles all patient-facing communication including chats, notifications, and appointment bookings.
AApplication Layer
AI Engine
Powers intelligent scheduling and conversational flows.
Schedule Management
Manages immunization schedules based on national guidelines.
Data Analytics
Provides insights into compliance and operational efficiency.
Microservices
Includes services for WhatsApp, AI/NLP, Scheduling, Notifications, Patients, Immunizations, Appointments, and Analytics.
DData & Integration Layer
Hospital Systems
Integrates with existing HIS/EMR, appointment, and billing systems.
Patient DB (PostgreSQL)
Stores patient profiles and medical history.
Schedule DB (MongoDB)
Manages dynamic immunization schedules and rules.
Appointment DB (MySQL)
Handles all booking and appointment data.
Analytics DB (ClickHouse)
Stores data for performance and compliance analytics.
